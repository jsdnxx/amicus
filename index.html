    About the brief

    On Sunday February 5th, 96 companies signed a legal brief in support of the State of Washington in their law suit opposing the #MuslimBan, the executive order prohibiting travel from 7 majority-Muslim countries, even for legal visa holders and permanent residents. The Executive Order is unlawful and unconstitutional, goes against the values and customs of the United States, and hurts the economic competitiveness of American companies and their ability to recruit and retain top skilled individuals.

    These companies recognized the unique threat posed by the reckless and immoral Executive Order, and use their legal resources to do something about it. If you work for a company that's not on the list, please talk to your fellow employees. Email your CEO and ask them to speak out and use their voice to unequivocally say no to discrimination and to support their employees and customers.

    This has been covered on The Verge, TechCrunch, and others.
